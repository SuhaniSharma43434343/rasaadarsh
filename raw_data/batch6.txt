81. Hridayarnav Rasa (हृदयार्णव रस)


Chapter: Hridaya Chikitsa (Treatment of Heart Diseases). Indicated for Kapha-origin heart disease (Kaphajanit Hridaya Roga).

Sanskrit Shlokas
Shloka 1
Shuddhasootasamam gandhyam mritataamram tayoh samam | Mardyetrifalakaatheh kaakamaacheedraverddinam ||1||
Shloka 2
Chanamaatram vatim khaadedraso ayam hridayaarnavah | Kaakamaacheephalam karpam triphalaaphalasamyutam ||2||
Shloka 3
Dvaatrimshatolakam toyam kvaathamashhtaavaseshitam | Anupaanem pibechchaatra hridrogam cha kaphotthite ||3||

Take Shu. Parada (purified mercury) and Shu. Gandhaka (purified sulphur) in equal parts, and Tamra Bhasma (copper ash) equal to the combined weight of both. Triturate all three together with Triphala decoction and Kakamachi (Solanum nigrum) juice — for one full day each. Form pills of Chana-matra (chickpea size). This is Hridayarnav Rasa. Taking one such pill cures Kaphajanit Hridaya Roga (Kapha-origin heart disease). Anupana: Take Kakamachi fruit (Makoy), Hara (Haritaki), Baheda (Vibhitaka) and Amla (Amalaki) — 1 Karsha (about 12g) each — boil in 32 tolas of water until 4 tolas remain. Filter and drink this decoction as Anupana after the pill.

4. Form pills of Chana-matra (chickpea size, approx. 125–250 mg). Dry in shade and store in glass vessel. This is Hridayarnav Rasa.

Dosage & Administration
Dose: 1 pill (Chana-matra size) daily. Anupana: Triphala-Kakamachi decoction (as described above).

100. Chandra Shekhar Ras (चन्द्रशेखर रसः)
Chapter: Nirama Jwara (Fever without ama/toxins). Reference: Classical Ayurvedic texts. Classical number [61]. Indicated for Nirama Jwara (fever without chills/toxins).
Sanskrit Shloka
Shloka 61
Vihaaya sharkaram yada pradeeyate manah-shilaa |
Tadaa niraamakajvaraariresha chandrashekhara: || 61 ||
When Manashila (Realgar/Arsenic disulphide) is given in place of Sharkara (sugar) in the Udakamanjari Ras combination, and the tablet is prepared by grinding as before, then this preparation — Chandra Shekhar Ras — destroys Nirama Jwara (fever without ama/toxins). Dose: 1/4 Ratti to 3/4 Ratti (15 mg to 60 mg).
Ingredients
Ingredient (Sanskrit)	English Name	Quantity
Udakamanjari Ras (उदकमञ्जरी रस)	Udakamanjari herbal preparation/decoction	As base (principal)
Manashila (मनःशिला)	Realgar (Arsenic Disulphide — As2S2)	In place of Sharkara (sugar)
Sharkara (शर्करा) [replaced]	Sugar (replaced by Manashila in this variant)	Replaced

Method of Preparation (Vidhi)
1.	Take the Udakamanjari Ras base preparation.
2.	In place of Sharkara (sugar), add Manashila (purified Realgar/Arsenic Disulphide).
3.	Grind (Ghont) all ingredients together as before (Purvavat) to form Vati (tablets).
4.	Dry in shade and store in glass. This is Chandra Shekhar Ras.
Therapeutic Benefits
Chandra Shekhar Ras destroys Nirama Jwara — fever occurring without ama (undigested toxins), i.e., straightforward fever without chills or ama-associated complications.
