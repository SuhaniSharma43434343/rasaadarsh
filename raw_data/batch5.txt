2. Give 1 Bhavana with Pippali Kashaya (long pepper decoction). Dry.
3. Give 1 Bhavana with Snuhi Ksheera (Euphorbia milk). Dry.
4. Form tablets of 375 mg each. Dry in shade and store in glass. This is Vidyadhara Ras.

Dosage & Administration
Dose: 375 mg morning and evening with Madhu (honey).
After taking the tablet, drink 50 ml Gau Mutra (cow's urine).

Therapeutic Benefits
Vidyadhara Ras with Madhu and Gau Mutra treats significantlys: Gulma (abdominal tumor — all types) and Pleeha Roga (spleen disorders including splenomegaly).
