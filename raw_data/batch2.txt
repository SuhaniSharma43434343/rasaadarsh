Amrit Vati is made of equal parts of Kushtha (Saussuraea costus), Gandhaka (sulphur), Visha (Vatsanabhi/aconite), Vyoma (Trikatu — the three pungents), Trifala (Haritaki, Baheda, Amla) and Parada (mercury) — ground and formed into tablets with Bhringaraja juice. This excellent Vati treats significantly Ajirna (indigestion), Shlesham (Kapha) and Vata diseases. It is an extremely powerful Deepan (digestive fire stimulant) and Ruchivardhak (appetite enhancer).

It produces powerful Deepana, Pachana, treats significantly Dushta Aam (toxic undigested matter), excessive body fat and obesity, removes poison/toxic effects, treats significantly Gulma (abdominal tumor), Pleeha Roga (spleen disorders), Grahani (malabsorption), Vatagranthi (Vata nodules) and Mahodara (severe abdominal distension).

Method of Preparation (Vidhi)
1. Make proper Kajjali from Parada and Gandhaka. Add Tamra and Loha Bhasma and Triturate briefly.
2. Take 2-3 kg fresh cow dung, form cylindrical ball (one Vitasti wide, 3-4 fingers thick). Place 3-4 Eranda (castor) leaves on it. Wrap 250 gm cow dung in Eranda leaves to make separate bundle.

16. Raksha Ras (राक्षसरस)


Sanskrit Shlokas
Shloka 140
Taamram paaradagandhakau trikatukam teekshmam cha souvarchalam khalle mardya dridham vidhaaya sikatakumbheShtayaaman tatah | Svidhram tasya cha raktashaakini bhavam kshaaram samam melayeta sarvam bhaavitamatulungajarasairnamna raso raakhasah ||140||
Take Tamra (copper), Parada (mercury), Gandhaka (sulphur), Trikatu (three pungents), Tiksna Loha (sharp iron) and Souvarchala (black salt) — Triturate firmly in a mortar, then fire in a sand-filled pot for eight Yamas (24 hours). After cooling, take its Rakta Shakini Bhava Kshar (a specific alkali derived from it) and mix equal quantity of all. Give Bhavana with Matulunga Nimbu Svaras (citron/lemon juice). This formulation is named Raksha Ras.
Shloka 141
Mandaagnau satatam dadeet munaye praatah puraa shankurah sakhye'smai chyavanaaya mandahuta bhugavyaayai nashtaujase | Tenaadaaya samasta loka guruve sooryaaya tasmai namah | Martyaanaamapi chaasya daanasamaye gunjaashtak vaarjayet ||141||
This Ras should always be given for Mandagni. Long ago, Lord Shankur (Shiva) gave this medicine to his friend Mahishi Chyavana Muni, who suffered from Mandagni, weakened Agni and depleted Ojas. Chyavana Muni then revealed it to the world for the benefit of all. We bow to the Sun — the great teacher of the world. When giving this medicine to mortals, do not give more than eight Gunja (1 gm) at a time.

Ingredients (Ghatak)
Ingredient (Sanskrit)	English Name	Quantity
Tamra Bhasma (ताम्रभस्म)	Incinerated Copper 	Equal part
Shu. Parada (शु. पारद)	Purified Mercury	Equal part
Shu. Gandhak (शु. गन्धक)	Purified Sulphur	Equal part
Trikatu Churna (त्रिकटुचूर्ण)	Ginger + Black Pepper + Long Pepper powder	Equal part
Tiksna Loha Bhasma (तीक्ष्णलोहभस्म)	 Incinerated Iron	Equal part
Souvarchala Lavana (सौवर्चललवण)	Black Salt	Equal part
Rakta Punarnava Kshar (रक्तपुनर्नवाक्षार)	Alkali of Red Boerhavia diffusa	Equal part
Matulunga Nimbu Svaras (मातुलुङ्गनिम्बुस्वरस)	Citron/Lemon juice — for Bhavana	As required

Method of Preparation (Vidhi)
1. In a stone mortar, firmly Triturate Parada and Gandhaka to make proper Kajjali.
2. Add Tamra Bhasma and remaining powders. Triturate well.
3. Fill this powder in a clay-sealed Kachkupi (glass bottle) and cook in Baluka Yantra (sand bath) for 24 hours.
4. When cooled, remove the medicine. Triturate in mortar and add equal quantity of Rakta Punarnava Kshar.
5. Give 7 Bhavanas with Matulunga Nimbu Svaras (lemon/citron juice). Triturate well.
6. Form tablets of 500 mg. Dry in shade and store in glass. This is called Raksha Ras.

Scholarly Commentary (Vimarsha)
Note: When processed in a glass bottle (Kachkupi) in Baluka Yantra, the Parada + Gandhaka will become Rasa Sindura (red mercuric sulphide). The remaining herbs will be destroyed by heat. Only Loha Bhasma will remain at the bottom. Therefore it is more appropriate to cook the medicine in a Sharava Samput (sealed earthen vessel) method instead of in a glass bottle.

Historical Significance
In ancient times, Lord Shankara (Shiva) gave this medicine to his devotee Maharishi Chyavana Muni, who was suffering from Mandagni (weak digestion), loss of Virya (vitality) and Ojas (vital essence). Maharishi Chyavana then revealed this medicine to the world for the welfare of all people. We bow to the Sun-like great teacher of the universe. This medicine should be used in doses of 1 Ratti to 8 Ratti. Consuming it treats significantly Ajirna Rog. At 1 Ratti dose, avoid Kakara Ashtaka (eight prohibited substances).

Therapeutic Benefits
Consumption of Raksha Ras treats significantly Ajirna Rog (indigestion disorders).

Properties

Color (Varna)	Kapotavarna (Pigeon grey)
Vehicle (Anupana)	Takra Anupana (buttermilk)
Taste (Svad)	Kshariya (Alkaline)
Smell (Gandha)	Rasayana Gandhi

17. Vadvagni Ras (वडवाग्निरस)


Sanskrit Shlokas
Shloka 105

Take separately three Karsha (30 gm each) of Tankana (borax), Maricha (black pepper) and Tutya (copper sulphate/blue vitriol). Take twelve Nishka weight of Sundara (a specific purified metal) and thirty-three portions of Mandoora (iron rust oxide).
Shloka 106

Kaanta Loha (magnetic iron) should be triturated with Padmarasa (lotus juice) and given Puta (firing). Then Incinerated Iron triturated with Bhringaraja (Eclipta alba) juice seven times.
Shloka 107
Choornanyetaani sanyojya sthaapayecchuddha bhaajane | Shuddhadeho narastasya paanam yadbhojanottaram ||107||
Combine all these powders and store in a clean vessel. This is to be consumed by a person who has undergone purification (Panchakarma/Shodhan). It should be taken after food.
Shloka 108
Adyaatpathyam tatah svalpam tatastaambuulabhaagbhavet | Udraagnirnasya asya vadavaagnisamo bhavet | Bahunaatra kimuttena rasaayanamayam nrinaam ||108||
The diet should start with what is wholesome, then gradually a little, then Tambula (betel). The gastric fire of this person will become equal to the Vadavagni (submarine fire — the most powerful fire in mythology). There is no need to say more — this is a Rasayana for mankind.

Ingredients (Ghatak)
Ingredient (Sanskrit)	English Name	Quantity
Shu. Tankana (शु. टङ्कण)	Purified Borax	35 gm
Maricha Churna (मरिचचूर्ण)	Black Pepper powder	35 gm
Shu. Tutya (शु. तुत्य)	Purified Copper Sulphate (Blue Vitriol)	35 gm
Kaanta Loha Bhasma (कान्तलोहभस्म)	Magnetic Incinerated Iron	36 gm

Method of Preparation (Vidhi)
1. Triturate Kaanta Loha Bhasma with Kamala Pushpa Ras (lotus flower juice) and give Gajaputa (firing). Give 7 Putas in this manner.
2. Then give Bhavana with Trifala Kwath and fire in Gajaputa 7 more times.
3. Mandura Bhasma should also be given Bhavana with Bhringaraja Svaras (Eclipta alba juice) and 7 Putas in Gajaputa, before adding to this formulation.
4. Combine all five processed ingredients together, Triturate thoroughly, and store in a glass container.
5. This is called Vadvagni Ras.

Dosage & Administration

Take Shuddha Parada (purified mercury) and Gandhaka (sulphur) in equal parts of one Karsha each. Make Kajjali by Triturateing firmly. Then add Gau Dugdha (cow's milk) and Triturate for one full day. Seal in a Musha (clay crucible) and cook.

Shloka 114
