151. Lokanath Ras (लोकनाथ रस)
Chapter: Pliha Roga Chikitsa (Treatment of Spleen Diseases). Indicated for Yakrit (liver), Pliha (spleen), Udara (abdominal disorders), Gulma (abdominal lump) and Shotha (oedema/swelling).

Sanskrit Shlokas
Shlokas 2–5
Paaradam gandhakashchaiva samabhaagam vimardayet |
Mrinaabhram rasatulyashcha punastatraiva mardayet || 2 ||
Rasaadwidgunalohashcha lohtulyashcha taamrakam |
Bhasma varatikaayas cha taamratrirgunam kuru || 3 ||
Naagavalleera senaiwa mardayedyakrutah bhishak |
Putadwaapute vidvaan svaangasheetan samuddharet || 4 ||
Yakritpleehohdaram gulmam shwayathum cha vinaashayet |
Pippalaamadhusanyuktaam sagudam vaa hareetakeem |
Gomutrashcha pibecchhaanu gudam vaa jeerakaan vitam || 5 ||

Take Shuddha Para (purified mercury) — 1 part, Shuddha Gandhaka (purified sulphur) — 1 part, Abhraka Bhasma — 1 part, Loha Bhasma — 2 parts, Tamra Bhasma — 2 parts and Kauri Bhasma (Incinerated Cowrie shell ) — 6 parts. Combine all, prepare Kajjali (black sulphide) from mercury and sulphur, then Triturate with Nagavalli (Paan/betel leaf) juice and heat in Gajaputa. Remove when cooled to body temperature and preserve. Administer with Pippali (long pepper) and honey, or with Harad (Haritaki) and Jaggery, or with Jeera (cumin) powder and Gomuthra (cow urine) or Jaggery.

Ingredients
Ingredient (Sanskrit)	English Name	Quantity
Shu. Parada (शु. पारद)	Purified Mercury	1 part
Shu. Gandhaka (शु. गन्धक)	Purified Sulphur	1 part
Abhraka Bhasma (अभ्रकभस्म)	Incinerated Mica	1 part
Loha Bhasma (लोहभस्म)	Incinerated Iron 	2 parts
Tamra Bhasma (ताम्रभस्म)	Incinerated Copper 	2 parts
Kauri Bhasma (कौड़ीभस्म)	Incinerated Cowrie shell 	6 parts
Nagavalli Svaras (नागवल्ली रस)	Betel leaf juice — for trituration	As required

Method of Preparation (Vidhi)
1.	Prepare Kajjali by Triturate Shu. Parada (1 part) and Shu. Gandhaka (1 part) together until a smooth black mass forms.
2.	Add Abhraka Bhasma (1 part), Loha Bhasma (2 parts), Tamra Bhasma (2 parts) and Kauri Bhasma (6 parts). Mix all thoroughly.
3.	Triturate with Nagavalli (betel leaf) juice until well mixed.
4.	Heat in Gajaputa (cowdung cake fire). Remove when cooled to body temperature.
5.	Form tablets. Dry in shade and store in glass. This is Lokanath Ras.

Therapeutic Benefits
Lokanath Ras treats significantlys: Yakrit Roga (liver diseases), Pliha Roga (spleen diseases), Udara Roga (abdominal disorders), Gulma (abdominal lump/tumour) and Shwayathu/Shotha (oedema/swelling).

Properties
Property	Value
Dose (Matra)	As prescribed
Vehicle (Anupana)	With Pippali + Honey; or Haritaki + Jaggery; or Jeera Churna + Gomuthra/Jaggery
Indication (Upayoga)	Yakrit (Liver), Pliha (Spleen), Udara, Gulma, Shotha (Oedema)

— End of Formulation 151: Lokanath Ras —

This Agnikumar Loha treats significantlys Yakrit (liver), Pliha (spleen), Udara Roga (abdominal diseases), Gulma and Halimaka.
