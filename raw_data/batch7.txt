Kantakariyavaksharalangaleeksharasaindhavam | Madhukasaram sancchurnaya saptaham chardraka dvavaih ||37||
Also take: Kantakari (Solanum surattense) alkali, Langali (Gloriosa superba) alkali, and Saindhava Lavana (rock salt). Powder all together and triturate for seven days with Ardraka (ginger) juice.

Gutikam badarakaram shleshmakasapanutaye | Bhakshayedbholabaddho'yam rasah sawasapandujit ||38||
Form pills the size of a small jujube (Badara). This Bolbaddha Ras, when consumed, treats significantlys Kapha-type Kasa (cough), Shwasa (asthma), and Pandu (anaemia).
