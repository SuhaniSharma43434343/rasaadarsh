181. Chandrakala Ras (चन्द्रकलारस)
Chapter: Raktapitta Chikitsa (Treatment of Haemorrhagic Disorders). Formulation number 4 in this chapter. Also known as 'Rasendra' (king of Rasas). Indicated for Raktapitta (haemorrhagic disorders — bleeding from various orifices), Pitta Roga (all Pitta disorders), Vata-Pitta Roga, Agni-mandya (weak digestion), Jwara (fever), Bhrama (vertigo), Murchha (fainting), Streenam Raktapradar (excessive menstrual bleeding in women), Urdhvadha Raktapitta (bleeding upward and downward), Raktavamana (haematemesis — blood vomiting), Mutra-kricchhra (dysuria/difficult urination). Especially effective in Grishma (summer) and Sharad (autumn) seasons.
